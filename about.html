About Cannon Mobile Mechanic LLC

Twenty-plus years
under the hood.

Cannon Mobile Mechanic LLC was built on one idea: a properly equipped mechanic doesn't need a shop address to do shop-quality work. We bring the diagnostic scanner, the lift-free tooling, and the know-how straight to your driveway, your job site, or the shoulder of the road.

Our Story

Started with a truck, a toolbox, and a phone number.

Cannon Mobile Mechanic LLC is based out of Riddleton, Tennessee, and grew out of two decades of hands-on work across electrical systems, engine and transmission repair, and general automotive diagnostics. Instead of asking customers to find a way to a shop, tow a dead vehicle, or wait days for a bay to open up, we built a mobile operation that carries professional-grade diagnostic and repair equipment to wherever the vehicle already is.

Today we cover a 50-mile radius around Riddleton, working on everything from daily commuter cars and diesel trucks to classic and antique vehicles that need a mechanic who still understands carburetors, points, and mechanical fuel pumps — not just what a scan tool tells them.
