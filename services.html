Engine Repair & Diagnostics

From an intermittent misfire to a full internal rebuild, we diagnose off of live data and freeze-frame — not just a code lookup.

  • OBD-II & manufacturer-specific diagnostic scanning
  • Check-engine light & misfire diagnosis
  • Timing belt / timing chain replacement
  • Head gasket & cylinder head service
  • Valve train service (lifters, rockers, VVT components)
  • Fuel injection service & injector replacement
  • Ignition system service (coils, plugs, wires)
  • Water pump, thermostat & cooling system repair
  • Oil leak diagnosis (valve cover, oil pan, rear main)
  • Turbocharger & forced-induction diagnostics
  • Emissions system repair (EGR, EVAP, catalytic)
  • Full or partial engine rebuild and replacement

Transmission Repair & Service

Automatic, manual, and CVT diagnostics and repair — from routine fluid service to clutch and valve body work.

  • Automatic transmission diagnostics & repair
  • Manual transmission & clutch replacement
  • Transmission fluid flush & filter service
  • Shift solenoid & valve body diagnostics
  • Torque converter service & replacement
  • Clutch master & slave cylinder replacement
  • CV axle & driveshaft repair
  • Transfer case & 4WD/AWD system diagnostics
  • Shift cable & linkage adjustment
  • Slipping, hard-shifting & delayed-engagement diagnosis

Electrical & Module Programming

Wiring, charging systems, and computer-level module and key programming — the systems most shops farm out, we handle directly.

  • Battery, starter & alternator testing and replacement
  • Wiring harness diagnosis & repair
  • Parasitic draw & short-circuit tracing
  • ECU / PCM programming & reflashing
  • Module programming (BCM, TCM, ABS, airbag)
  • Key fob & remote start programming
  • CAN-bus & network communication diagnostics
  • ABS & SRS (airbag) system diagnostics
  • TPMS sensor programming & relearn
  • Lighting, gauge cluster & power accessory repair

Suspension & Steering

Ride quality, handling, and steering-component work — including lift and leveling installs for trucks.

  • Strut & shock absorber replacement
  • Control arm & bushing replacement
  • Ball joint & tie rod end replacement
  • Wheel bearing & hub assembly replacement
  • Sway bar link & bushing replacement
  • Power steering rack & pump service
  • Coil spring & leaf spring replacement
  • Lift kit & leveling kit installation
  • Clunk, pull & vibration diagnosis
  • Pre-alignment component inspection*

*We replace worn components on-site; final wheel alignment is performed at a partner alignment rack.

  • Mechanical fuel pump replacement
  • Distributor service & timing adjustment
  • Generator-to-alternator conversions
  • Mechanical brake system service
  • Engine rebuild & mechanical restoration support
  • Pre-show & pre-sale mechanical inspection

Roadside & On-Site Repair

Stuck in a lot, a driveway, or on the shoulder — we come to the vehicle instead of the vehicle coming to a tow truck.

  • Jump starts & battery testing
  • On-site battery replacement
  • No-start & no-crank diagnosis
  • Belt & hose replacement
  • Alternator & starter swaps on-site
  • Fluid leak diagnosis & top-off
  • Fuse, relay & electrical fault checks
  • Pre-purchase & pre-trip mechanical inspection
Not offered: air conditioning service.

We do not perform A/C diagnostics, recharge, or repair — we don't carry refrigerant recovery/recharge equipment. Ask us for a local referral if you need A/C work done.
